Coldplay

About Coldplay

Coldplay is a British rock band formed in London in 1996. The band consists of Chris Martin (lead vocals, piano, guitar), Jonny Buckland (guitar, keyboards), Guy Berryman (bass), and Will Champion (drums, percussion, piano, backing vocals).

Coldplay has released numerous successful albums, including "Parachutes," "A Rush of Blood to the Head," "X&Y," "Viva la Vida or Death and All His Friends," and "Mylo Xyloto."

Discography

  • Parachutes: Released in 2000
  • A Rush of Blood to the Head: Released in 2002
  • X&Y: Released in 2005
  • Viva la Vida or Death and All His Friends: Released in 2008
  • Mylo Xyloto: Released in 2011
  • Ghost Stories: Released in 2014
  • A Head Full of Dreams: Released in 2015
  • Everyday Life: Released in 2019
  • Music of the Spheres: Released in 2021

Awards and Achievements

Coldplay has received numerous awards and accolades throughout their career, including seven Grammy Awards, nine Brit Awards, and six MTV Video Music Awards. They have also been named one of the world's best-selling music artists, with over 100 million records sold worldwide.
